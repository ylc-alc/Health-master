4-Week Personalised Workout Planner

Built for a mat, one 1.5 kg dumbbell, and short home sessions. Each workout follows a fixed weekly sequence, with the main block designed for about 25 to 30 minutes. Warm-up and cool-down sit outside that main block.

4 weeks 4 training days per week Main block 25 to 30 min No bench or chair needed

Programme rules

  • Use the single Start workout button in the next workout card.
  • The planner shows one week at a time to keep the page lighter.
  • Future days stay locked until the current session is completed.
  • The programme moves by sequence, not by calendar date, so missed days do not break it.
